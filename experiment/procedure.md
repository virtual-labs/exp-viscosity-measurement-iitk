<b>Calculation and determination of the concentration of the unknown solution</b>
<br>
A.	Note down the weight of empty pycnometer, pycnometer with water and pycnometer with known and unknown sugar samples and calculate the    density of each sugar samples.
B.	Note down the time of flow of sugar solutions in the viscometer.
C.	Calculate the viscosity of each sugar solution from the formula.
D.	Plot the viscosities against the concentration of sugar solutions and from the plot determine the concentration of unknown solution.
